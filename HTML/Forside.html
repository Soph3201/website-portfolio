About Me

My name is Sophie Østergaard Knudsen and I am a student of multimedia designer in Nykøbing F. For as long as I can remember, I have always been very interested in the creative, which is why I love all the graphics. My free time is spent on everything from sketching to various projects, to painting, embroidering, jewelry, nail technique and much more. I think it's really cool when an idea unfolds into a good product that you can share with others. I also believe that all ideas are good ideas, it's just about how you get them out into the world.


I consider myself a perfectionist, and although it may at times seem like an enemy, I see it as a strength. It's a strength that makes me always give myself fully in my projects, as I am really productive, constructive and can be self-critical when needed. Furthermore, I consider myself an introverted person who can always vouch for what she loves to do.


Good friends and colleagues are important to me as I love sharing my knowledge and interest with others. In addition, I love to try and develop new skills. I am therefore always ready to get to know new people.
