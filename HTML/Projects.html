Nykøbing Falster rowing club is a rowing club concept I have developed together with some fellow students. Here, my role was to find statistics that could substantiate our knowledge, ideas for a possible concept, creating personas, Isketching for our front page, about-page, event-page, picture-page and registration-page and to code the NFR image page.

We did not really have any challenges with the project other than that it has been difficult to make more modern without changing too much.

Den Hurtige Skildpadde is a karate club that I along with some fellow students had been tasked with redesigning. The concept was based on Japanese culture which is especially seen by the colors on the page and the logo.

My task in the project was to find statistics , make moodboard, personas and sketch for our redesign of logo. In the logo there was a focus on the Japanese flag and the turtle shield with a belt on as it should symbolize the martial art. I also got to code Den Hurtige Skildpaddes about page.

Our challenge with the project was that it was not our own code we were sitting with, which was difficult to work with as they had not coded in the same way as we would have done.

Oceancase is a mobile cover concept that I have developed with some fellow students. Oceancase focus on making phone cases from recycling of plastic.

My task was to find the trends of the time, make shoting lists for our 2 advertising videos, find statistics , figure out what times it was best to post in and to code our Samsung page with sales products.

Our challenge in the project was a lot of coding, as we wanted to make a really cool webshop page, which we had difficulty coding. In addition we did not make it responsive. We also had challenges when we were going out to film, as we had expected to find a lot of rubbish on a beach but basically did not find anything. But it blew a lot that day and it was cold which made it difficult to get filmed in longer time. We were also a bit under time pressure in the end, so we decided that we did not have time to photoshop more shop photos as it takes a long time.
